Q. Understanding of thread and process, difference between both.

ANSWER:

	Process means any program is in execution while thread means only the segment of process is in execution.

	Process takes more time to create, switch as well as terminate while thread takes less times comparitively.
	Process do not share resources and is totally independent, thread shares resources.
	System calls can be invoked in process, while cannot be invoked in thread.
	Process have overhead while thread do not have overhead.
